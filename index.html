Who Are We

Spark Data Impact (SDI) is a company specializing in big data analytics and IT solutions.
IDG focuses on helping businesses and organizations leverage data to drive growth,
innovation, and informed decision-making. By offering a range of services, including
data analytics, enterprise resource planning (ERP), and cloud-based solutions, IDG enables
